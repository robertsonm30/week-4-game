Diamond Collector!

You Will Be Given A Random Number At The Start Of The Game.

There are Four Diamonds Below. By Clicking On The Diamond You Will add A Specific Amount Of Points To Your Total Score.

You Win The Game By Matching Your Total Score To The Random Number, You Lose The Game If Your Total Score Goes Above The Random Number.

The Value Of Each Diamond Is Hidden From You Until You Click On It. Deterimne The Value Of Each Diamond Based On The Increase In Your Score.

Each Time When The Game Starts, The Game Will Change The Value Of The Diamonds.
